Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal 'Crown of thePalace', is an ivory-white marble mausoleum on the southern bank of the river Yamuna in the Indian city of Agra.It was commissioned in 1632 by the Mugal emperor Shah Jahan(reigned from 1628 to 1658) to house the tomb of his favourite wife, Mumtaz Mahal; it also housesthe tomb of Shah Jahan himself. The tomb is the centerpiece of a 17-hectare(42-acre) complex, which include a mosque and a guest house, and is set in formal gardens bounded on three sides by a crenellated wall.
