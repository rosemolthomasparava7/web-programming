GOA BEACH

Let the sea and the Sun kiss your soul as you spend pensive hours on Arambol Beach. A handful of shacks offer unforgettable sea food and a mix of cuisines to delight you. Quite close to the Dabolim Airport, this is one of those beaches in Goa that see ardent beach lovers in winters. Perfect for a sunny day out, Arambol is where you can engage in a game of volleyball at the beach.
